Inject following variables:
  • JOB_OWNER - UserId of the Job's owner
  • JOB_OWNER_EMAIL - E-mail of the Job's owner
  • JOB_COOWNERS - Comma-separated list of the Job's owners (includes owner)
  • JOB_COOWNERS_EMAILS - Comma-separated list of e-mails of the job owners(includes owner's e-mail)
All variables may be empty.
